My name is Delsie Ivy or better known as Dele. I am the owner of a small business called ResinByDele. Comes from a Kadazan Labuk family, I speak three languages: Malay, English and Kadazan Labuk. ResinByDele is the making of craft using resin. On my free time, I love to search all over the internet to find something that I can sell. Because of that, I am now selling resin craft where I can put all my creativity then sell it.
